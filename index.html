Biography

I studied the PhD at the Universidad Nacional Autonoma de Mexico in the program of Biomedicine at Posgrado en Ciencias Biologicas. My research is focused on studying the underlying mechanisms associated with chemotherapy resistance in cancer samples using own and publicly RNA-seq data.
